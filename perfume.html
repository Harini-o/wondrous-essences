About Us



Our Mission


At Wondrous Essences, we're passionate about crafting extraordinary fragrances that evoke emotions and inspire connection. We believe in the transformation power of scent and strive to create unique, high-quality perfumes that transport you to a world of wonder.


Our Story


Founded in 2024 by Harini Priya, Wondrous Essences began with simple idea:to create fragrances that capture the essence of life's most precious moments. With a passion for perfumery and a commitment to empowerment, Harini assembled an all-female team, F1 Girls, to revolutionize the fragrance industry.


Meet Our Team


Harini priya ,Founder & CEO

Harini's fascination with fragrance begn with her love for natural ingredients and essential oils. With a visin to empower women through scent, Harini created Wondrous Essences.
